Design Principles

3 Principles of Design by Agnes

03
1

/Balance/
Symmetrical balance is when elements which is used on one side of the design are similar to the other side.
Asymmetrical balance is when the sides are different but still look balanced.

2

/Repetition/
Repetition is done by putting together individual elements. It creates consistency and makes a rhythm. It is also a fantastic tool for leading eyes across a page.

3

/Contrast/
Contrast is basically opposite colours on the colour wheel. It helps emphasise and highlight important elements in the design.
